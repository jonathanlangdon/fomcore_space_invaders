Console turns on automatically with power, but you can also press the power button if that doesn't work.

double click on the green fomcore-invaders icon to get started

controllers are wireless, but keep one plugged in while using the other so that you'll always have one fully charged. 

press 'start' on the controller to turn on

At the screen where you can enter your name, use the keyboard.  After you're finished either press enter or a button on the controller

The Yellow button fires bullets

At any time press ESC on the keyboard to exit the game

If the high score needs to be reset, exit the game and double click on the "Reset High Score" icon on the desktop
